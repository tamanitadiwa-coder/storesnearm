Privacy Policy

StoresNearMe  ·  Effective: April 2025

Overview

Your Privacy Matters

StoresNearMe is built on a simple principle — your data exists to serve you, not us. This policy outlines exactly what we collect, why we collect it, and how it remains protected at all times.

01

Information We Collect

  • Device location data — used only in real time to find stores near you. We do not store or log your location history.
  • Anonymous usage signals — aggregated, non-identifiable data (screen views, feature interactions) to improve app performance.
  • Search queries — temporarily processed to return relevant results; never linked to your identity.
02

How We Use Your Information

Your location is used solely to surface nearby stores in real time. Usage signals help us identify and fix friction in the experience. We never build profiles, run targeted advertising, or retain personal data beyond the active session.

03

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. Mapping and store data is fetched from public APIs (OpenStreetMap / Overpass) under open-data licenses. No personal identifiers are transmitted in these requests.

04

Data Security

All network communication is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. We apply the principle of minimum data collection — if we don't need it, we don't touch it. Your location is processed on-device where possible and is never written to persistent storage.

05

Your Rights & Controls

  • Revoke location access at any time via your device settings.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes any locally cached data.
  • Request deletion of any data associated with your use by contacting us directly.
06

Children's Privacy

StoresNearMe is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with data, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.
